About YCCE

Yeshwantrao Chavan College 10h March 2022 of Engineering (YCCE) was established in the year 1984 by Nagar Yuvak Shikshan Sanstha, Nagpur. It is an autonomous institution affiliated to Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur. For over 36 years, it has successfully nurtured young YCCE is the First private Engineering College to acquire "Autonomous Status" in central India. The institute is awarded with 'A' Grade by National ASsessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). YCCE has the vision to become the most preferred institution providing innovative, research and value based, professional education for the society at large and by the Academic Advisory Board consisting of eminent academicians from the prestigious technical institutes in India and USA.


About CIVIL DEPARTMENT OF ENGINEERING

The Department is accredited three times since 2003 by the National Board of Accreditation. The Primary objective of department is to become a well known centre for pursuing Civil Engineering education through innovative pedagogy, value-based education and industry collaboration. It provides facility to conduct research at the Undergraduate, Post graduate and Doctoral levels in various areas of Civil Engineering with emphasis on emerging areas of Structural engineering, Environmental engineering, Hydraulics engineering, Soil engineering, Transportation engineering, Concrete technology. The department has well qualified and experienced senior faculty members.
